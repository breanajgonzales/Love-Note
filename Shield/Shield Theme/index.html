A LITTLE ABOUT LOVENOTE


You’re reading this because you’re a hopeless romantic… or your wife made you. Either way we are glad someone in your life thinks love is important.

Here’s the thing: Love - at least the good kind of love - is not easy, because life just gets too complicated. For example, how often do you find yourself sacrificing spousal sexy-times for PTA meetings, barf cleanups, or work deadlines? Somehow date nights have mutated from a night of fun and connection to the only 2 hours of blissful silence you get in a month… or a week if you’re lucky.

This isn’t what you signed up for.

We think you deserve better.

Here at LoveNote we want to give you all the tools you need to make you and your spouse feel like one million dollars. We’ll remind you to say thank you for the little things, like popping each other’s back zits. We won’t let you forget the important stuff, like that foot rubs make her horny, and surprising him with his favorite lunch will result in the dishwasher being emptied without complaint.

Every day is an opportunity to grow together, and we will proudly be your fertilizer.
